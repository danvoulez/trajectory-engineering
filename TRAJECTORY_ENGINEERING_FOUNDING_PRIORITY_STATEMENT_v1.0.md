> **Priority statement.** Trajectory Engineering was first formulated and developed by Dan Voulez at The LogLine Foundation as the discipline of transforming lived, admitted histories of action into compiled, sealed, verifiable and invocable attestation assets while preserving custody of the underlying trajectory.

This document publicly establishes the name, thesis, economic mechanism and open/proprietary boundary of Trajectory Engineering. It does not disclose the proprietary methods used to analyze the fold or determine whether a trajectory was lived or fabricated.

## Thesis

A trajectory is not merely training material for AI. Training is the particular case. The general case is this: **a lived trajectory, compiled into a sealed binary, is an attestation asset.**

A compiled trajectory carries a manifest and can be RUN. It answers questions. It is, in essence, a CLI with auth: **the auth varies, the information varies.** No one accesses the data; everyone interrogates the seal.

Trajectory Engineering therefore concerns the complete transformation:

```text
lived acts
→ admitted history
→ trajectory
→ fold
→ sealed compilation
→ limited attestation
→ remuneration without transfer of custody
```

## The use case that pays

Insurers, audit firms and banks — the heavyweights of any economy — live off trust, and they carry a permanent problem: a client whose history their own systems cannot authenticate.

They can turn to a **trajectory market** and use binaries holding authentic trajectories of lived human life.

```text
Two binaries together: one attests the other, revealing nothing.
They emit only a "green".
```

The company is satisfied. The client is satisfied. And the **owner of the trajectory** used in the verification is remunerated — simply for having their trajectory registered and available in a bank. A lived life becomes an annuity.

The trajectory does not need to be a success story. By the mere fact of having been lived, it carries **folds** — creases bearing the traces of human life that synthetic data cannot forge in depth: hesitation, retries, corrections, the time between acts, doubt genuinely exercised and changes of course.

## The open / proprietary split

```text
OSS — The LogLine Foundation:
    the flat trajectory — chained JSON without fold — and its binary form.
    Anyone can emit, chain and verify form.

Proprietary house runtime:
    analysis of the FOLD — the structural authenticity of lived life.
    Only the house runtime judges whether a trajectory was lived or fabricated.
```

The open format creates the market. The closed analysis captures the margin.

The public contribution establishes the interoperable object and its verifiable form. The proprietary contribution preserves the method by which the structural authenticity and economic fitness of the trajectory are judged.

## Original claims established by this statement

1. **A lived trajectory can be compiled into a sealed binary attestation asset.**
2. **The asset can be interrogated without transferring or exposing its underlying trajectory.**
3. **Two sealed trajectories can participate in peer attestation and emit a limited result, including a single `green`, with zero disclosure of raw payload.**
4. **The owner of a trajectory can be remunerated for authorized availability and attestation without surrendering ownership or custody.**
5. **The economically scarce property is not merely recorded content, but the fold produced by lived temporal structure: hesitation, repetition, correction, doubt, delay, consequence and change of direction.**
6. **An open trajectory format and a proprietary fold-analysis runtime can form a coherent industrial boundary: the format creates the market; the fold captures the margin.**
7. **Trajectory Engineering names the discipline concerned with producing, preserving, compiling, interrogating and economically activating these assets.**

## Doctrinal anchors

- The seal verifies everything and extracts nothing — Arrow's information paradox, resolved at the source, promoted here from defensive property to **primary product**.
- The fractal law again: the card is to the worker what the Diamond is to the market — and the format is to the world what the fold is to the house.
- The vault fills with money by mechanics, not hope: sealed trajectories earn by availability, and the release gate charges per attestation.
- The fragile link remains the declared one: the runtime boundary. Firewall, leakage check and attestation remain the defense of the entire economic premise.
- Provenance is not quality, a receipt is not truth, and a seal is not correctness. Each proves a different property and must remain separately testable.

## What it requires — names, not plans

1. **Peer attestation protocol** — two binaries, one green, zero leakage.
2. **Canon of the fold** — what the record must preserve so the fold remains analyzable. The flat chain smooths away exactly what carries value; the rich canon is the one that **does not smooth**.
3. **Market registry** — state, availability, price and releases; never raw payload.
4. **Release gate** — authorization, pricing, purpose limitation and receipt for every invocation.
5. **Runtime boundary** — isolation, leakage testing and attestation sufficient to defend the premise that the seal can answer without surrendering its contents.
